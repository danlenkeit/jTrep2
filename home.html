About

Yes, we’d all like our special events and memories maintained and captured but at the expense of a complete distraction? How many here have been to a beautifully orchestrated event only to be constantly distracted by the video team?! Knowing that, you may rest assured that I will confidently capture and recreate your event while remaining unseen and un obtrusive. I have spent the last 10 years filming across the country in a wide range of scenarios. From corporate events, music videos, documentaries to Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, I have developed the skills necessary to create what my clients desire. Simple, elegant and impactful is the goal and It’s not just a video highlight. I believe that every client deserves a piece of art that represents the reality of the event they experienced. I will listen and work with you to create something meaningful for your whole family.

About Me:

My name is Jesse Trepiak and I am currently a Newton resident who grew up in upstate New York. Creative and visual arts have always been my passion and I have spent the last 10 years filming across the country capturing everything from corporate events and documentaries to music videos and Bar and Bat Mitzvahs. My mission is to run a customer-friendly, service-oriented business where my clients are part of the process and receive exactly the product they were expecting. Simple while elegant and impactful is my goal for every project.
